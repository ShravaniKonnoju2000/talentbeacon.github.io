OUR EXPERIENCE

Over a decade worth of experience working with major multinational companies, mid-size growth companies, and fast-growing startups who have depended on TalentBeacon for partnership-based hiring support and extensive experienced hiring and managing talent in India.

TalentBeacon's clients are a some of the best known global multinational corporations, as well as global and regional staffing companies, that work with us as a long-term trust partner for supplemental or fully outsourced recruitment, New company setup, and HR and Employer of Record consulting and support services.

India Based Recruitment Support

  • We do full and partial Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) services, bringing a high-end corporate approach where most India firms work in a very transactional agency recruiting model.
  • We have significant experience hiring and managing contractors in support of our client’s contingent labor requirements, either directly or through 3rd party MSA service management companies.
  • We support recruiting in international locations from India, with extensive experience servicing the Americas, EMEA, and the Indo-Asia regions.
  • We are deeply experienced in project based and longer term optimized RPO solution design, as well as execution oversight and metrics-based delivery management.
  • TalentBeacon also has experience implementing innovative supplemental IT interviewing and scalable screening services into solutions designs.

New Company Setup

  • TalentBeacon is one of the leading providers of “New Company Setup” services in India. Over the past decade, numerous international corporations have partnered with TalentBeacon successfully to enter the India market.
  • From growth companies to large multinationals, TalentBeacon has been a trusted, hands-on, and cost effective partner. When practical, firsthand experience is required to ensure that a new organization first year in India is successful, TalentBeacon’s is you best choice for ensuring a successful first 12 to 24 months.
  • We have comprehensive HR, Legal, Recruiting, Location selection and In-House Employer of Record and Payrolling experience to help companies avoid big consultancy setup costs, while ensuring that mission critical decisions, project planning, and deliverables execution are being managed with experienced insights, best practices, and risk avoidance discipline.

HR Consulting, Employer of Record and Payrolling Services

We have significant experience managing and consulting with international clients covering best practice HR management for India based companies. Our consulting services offer an “actual practitioner” approach to HR setup and management in India, vs. large consulting company “high level – high price” generalizations regarding best practices.

TalentBeacon also offers India based Employer of Record Services and Payrolling services to manage small to large employee groups. We cover best practice service delivery, government regulatory compliance, tax.

We use simple yet sophisticated HR service technology to manage and engage with our employees and ensure all documentation and record keeping is updated and available to our employees and clients.

Supplemental Service

TalentBeacon offers supplemental support services which are enormously beneficial for new companies' navigation India's various complexities. Our knowledge base and consulting services can help with guidance with:

  • Best domestic and international banking relationships.
  • Accounting firm relationships.
  • Law Firm Relationships.
  • M&A Relationships.
  • 1st, 2nd and 3rd tier city office selection and talent locations (+/- of each).
  • Real Estate management relationships.
